Rice

A simple dish that can accompany anything.

No rice cooker necessary. This is a staple dish and is very easy to prepare.

If you've followed any of my other recipes, you're probably wondering how such a basic dish could be Recipe for Disaster. Well, it's definitely one to chew on, so let's get cookin'!

Remember, this recipe is a Recipe for Disaster. There will be negative consequences if you do not read this carefully beforehand.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, optional

Steps

  1. Gather together the ingredients.
  2. Pour the 2 cups of water into a medium saucepan and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the salt and stir. Then, add the rice.
  4. Reduce heat, cover the saucepan and simmer.
  5. Check occasionally. When the water has evaporated, the rice is ready.
  6. Remove from heat, and serve on a plate.
  7. Take a pleasant bite of your homemade rice sans rice cooker, and proceed to munch down on a rock and break your tooth. How disastrous!

Looking to avoid disaster?

Maybe you don't like rocks in your food like the rest of us. No worries! Here's a tip to help you out.

Before cooking, rinse and sort through your rice. It takes an extra five minutes and even the lighter-colored rocks can be easily found.

Another suggestion, you may want to avoid any stone soup recipes in the future. Spoiler alert, it has a rock in it.
